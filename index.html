What this site is for

This site is part notebook, part workshop, part archive. I write about product judgment, startup execution, AI-assisted development, app ideas, and the habits and systems that shape modern work.

Some pieces will be polished essays. Others will be working notes, experiment logs, or project snapshots. The common thread is concrete learning from trying to build and understand things.
