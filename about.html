About

Product director, startup builder, and writer.

I’ve spent my career working across product, technology, and growth, with time at Amazon and in startup environments where clarity, judgment, and execution matter a lot.

This site is a place to share ideas about product leadership, company building, and the practical systems behind teams, tools, habits, and products that actually ship.

I’m especially interested in how good judgment gets built: through repeated decisions, better feedback loops, and clearer thinking over time.

The work here will range from professional essays to rougher project notes and experiments. The point is to make the thinking visible without turning the site into a resume or a personal brand machine.
